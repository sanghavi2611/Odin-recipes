Lasagna

Description

This is the best homemade lasagna you'll ever try. Layers of cheesy, meaty goodness that the whole family will love.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook ground beef, onion, and garlic until browned.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Boil lasagna noodles.
  5. Layer sauce, noodles, ricotta, and mozzarella.
  6. Repeat layers and finish with mozzarella.
  7. Bake for 30–40 minutes until bubbly.
