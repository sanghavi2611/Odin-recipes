Chicken Curry

Description

This classic Indian-style chicken curry is rich in flavor with aromatic spices, tender chicken, and a creamy tomato-based sauce. Best served with rice or naan.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup coconut milk or heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, and ginger; sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add spices and cook for 1–2 minutes to release their aroma.
  3. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomatoes and coconut milk. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Cook until chicken is fully cooked and sauce is thickened.
  6. Serve hot with rice or naan bread.
